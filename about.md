## About me

Hi, I'm Jack. 👋

I currently work as an independent contractor, largely focused on the development of the
[Rust programming language](https://www.rust-lang.org/). Generally, I focus heavily on the design and implemenation
of the type system, and on Project governance. I currently co-lead the types team, am a compiler team maintainer,
co-lead the [vision doc work](https://blog.rust-lang.org/2025/04/04/vision-doc-survey/), and am a Project Director on
the Rust Foundation Board.

I previously finished my Ph.D. in Biomedical Sciences in July 2025. My thesis work was on studying the role of the
Grainyhead-like family in the mouse thymus. But, I am broadly interested in the system-level gene regulation through
epigenetic and chromatin dynamics. I also have spent considerable time thinking about (biological) data processing,
visulaization, and accesibility.
